Face Functions

This was a Live-coding Collective Figure Drawing in-class exercise where students collaboratively coded segments of a figure in real-time, each responsible for a different part without seeing the others' contributions. This interactive session was a great way to enhance teamwork and coding skills, setting the stage for the complex and creative exquisite corpse project.

February 27 2024, Pratt Institute
