About Mistral Dotti

Mistral is an Italian multidisciplinary artist and designer based in New York. She specialises in Graphic Design with an emphasis on Ar, Vr and & 3D Design.

Mistral has graduated in 2021 from the Royal Melbourne Institute of Technology in Melbourne, Australia in Visual Communication Design with a minor in Marketing. She is currently completing an MFA in Graphic Design at Pratt Institute. From experimental publications to Co-Design, Information and Service Design, she approaches every project as a unique opportunity to apply hybrid design techniques to contemporary aesthetics.
