Glass Generator

In p5.js, I crafted a glass generator where I tailored variables and employed the random() function to set the range for each feature. By interacting with the mouse, users can explore various glass and fluid variations. Throughout the development process, I diligently tested, iterated, and refined every detail, deeply focusing on achieving precision and personal touch in the final output.
